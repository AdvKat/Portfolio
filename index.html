She works closely with project managers, engineers, and subcontractors to realize long-term objectives on construction projects and routine monitoring. She has experience using AutoCAD, ArcGIS Pro, Excel, and Adobe suite. She is known for an eagerness to learn, a high level of organization, and for actualizing and implementing creative solutions.
