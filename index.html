About Me

Katherine is a graduate student in the Geographic Information Science and Technology program at the University of California. The curriculum includes spatial data acquisition, spatial databases, ArcGIS, and remote sensing technologies.She is currently working as an environmental technician with a focus on groundwater remediation, her responsibilities include obtaining permits, client communications, and field leadership roles. She works closely with project managers, engineers, and subcontractors to accomplish long-term objectives on construction projects and routine monitoring. She has experience using AutoCAD, ArcGIS Pro, Excel, Adobe suite. She is known for an eagerness to learn, a high level of organization, and for implementing creative solutions.
